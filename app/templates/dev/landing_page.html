Jigyasa  

Hi there!!! I am Jigyasa

Problem Statement

Menstruation being treated as a taboo creates invisible boundaries that restrict females all over our nation and create negative impact on girls’ and women's emotional state, mentality and lifestyle and most importantly, health. There is a need to create awareness among women and girls that are unprepared and unaware of menstruation so they don't confront several difficulties and health issues at home, in schools, and at work.

So we came up with the idea of ‘Jigyasa’ as the SOLUTION

Jigyasa is a web based AI Voice Chatbot developed using Python Chatterbot that has been trained with questions girls and women can possibly have about menstruation and menstrual hygiene. Our society's mentality that menstrual hygiene is something to be discussed behind closed doors has costed many innocent women immensely. Jigyasa provides a safe space for girls and women to clarify their queries and to raise awareness amongst them.

Features of our project

Jigyasa is currently mono-lingual, i.e., it works with English.
Jigyasa also communicates with vocal input and output. This makes it serviceable for people that face difficulty in using digital technologies.
Jigyasa does not save the conversations with its users, providing confidentiality and thus a safe space for women to ask questions without needing to worry about disclosure. The chat clears the moment the chatbox collapses.
